Project Overview
Welcome to Quick Plate!
 This open-source platform is designed to empower restaurant owners by providing a centralized system to register their business, instantly generate a professional website, and efficiently manage their operations.
 Our goal is to make it easy for any restaurant, regardless of technical expertise, to establish a strong online presence and streamline their daily tasks.
